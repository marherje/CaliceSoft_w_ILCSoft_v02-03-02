Detailed Description

The Mokka conform cell index.

The cell index encodes the wafer column, and row, pad column and row, and the layer into one index. The wafer 0,0 is in the lower right corner (following a right handed coordinate frame ) looking in beam direction at the detector fron plate. Similar the pad 0,0 of each wafer is located in the lower right corner of the wafer. Encoding for the ECAL:

M:3,wafer row
S-1:3,wafer column
I:9,pad coloumn
J:9,pad row
K:6layer

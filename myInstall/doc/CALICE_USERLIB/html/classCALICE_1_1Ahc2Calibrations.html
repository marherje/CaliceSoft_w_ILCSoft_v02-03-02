Detailed Description

storing all informations necessary to calibrate a HBU SiPM read channel

This class is meant as central holder of all necessary informations to calibrate a HBU SiPM. Currently, following informations are stored:

  • MIP calibration in form of a LinearFitResult
  • gain calibration in form of a LinearFitResult
  • pedestal calibration in form of a SimpleValue
  • temperature calibration in form of a SimpleValue
  • inter calibration in form of a SimpleValue
  • inter calibration Physics Calib in form of a SimpleValue
  • saturation parameters in form of a SaturationParameters
  • time slopes parameters in form of a SimpleValueVector
  • time pedestal parameters in form of a SimpleValueVector
  • status in form of an integer

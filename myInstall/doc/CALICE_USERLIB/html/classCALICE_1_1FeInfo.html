int  sync_words_23,

Set the four frame sync out half words.

Parameters
sync_words_01the first and the second half word packed in one 32bit integer.
sync_words_23the third and the forth half word packed in one 32bit integer.
sync_word_4the fifth half word packed in the lower 16 bits of a 32bit integer.

The first word should be in the lower 16 bit of the first argument, the second word in the upper 16 bits of the first argument and so forth: 

sync_words_01= (word_0 & 0xffff) + ((word_1 & xffff)<<16);
